PARTS OF A STOMACH-
The stomach is the main organ of the digestive system. The oesophagus (food pipe) brings the food to the stomach and it later divides itself into nutrients and waste. The nutrients go around the body and the waste gets excreted from the anus.

ORGANS OF THE SYSTEM:
The digestive system consists of all organs involved in the process of food getting digested. These include mouth, oesophagus, salivary glands, small and large intestines, pancreas and anus.

TRANSPORT OF FOOD-
The food we eat travels from our mouth, to our oesophagus which goes to the stomach and then the intestines which gets segregated into nutrients and waste. The waste is excreted through the anus and the nutrients go all over the body.
